Our Story in Nkubu, Meru

EcoFarms Meru began as a small family farm dedicated to preserving traditional, organic coffee farming methods. What started as a passion project has grown into a thriving organic coffee farm and educational center.

Today, we welcome visitors from around the world to experience authentic Kenyan coffee culture and learn sustainable farming practices.
