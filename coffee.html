Our Organic Coffee Journey

From seed to your cup - the EcoFarms way

1

Seed Selection & Planting

We start with premium Arabica seeds selected for flavor and disease resistance. Planted in rich volcanic soil at optimal altitude (1,600-1,800m).

2

Organic Cultivation

Using compost from farm waste, natural pest control, and shade-grown techniques to nurture healthy plants without chemicals.

3

Hand Harvesting

Only ripe cherries are selectively picked by hand during the main harvest (October-January) and fly crop (May-July).

4

Natural Processing

Cherries are sun-dried on raised beds for even airflow, then hulled to reveal the green coffee beans inside.

5

Quality Sorting

Beans are meticulously sorted by size, density, and color to ensure only the best make it to roasting.

6

Artisan Roasting

Small-batch roasted to perfection to highlight each coffee's unique characteristics and flavor profile.
