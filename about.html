Our Organic Journey

From a small family farm to a center for sustainable coffee farming

The EcoFarms Story

Rooted in tradition, growing with innovation

1995

Humble Beginnings

Started as a small 2-acre family farm growing coffee using traditional organic methods passed down through generations.

2005

Certified Organic

Received official organic certification, committing to sustainable practices that protect the land and produce superior coffee.

2012

Educational Programs Begin

Started offering workshops to local farmers on organic techniques as demand for chemical-free farming grew.

2018

Farm Tours Launch

Opened our doors to visitors wanting to experience authentic Kenyan coffee culture and learn about sustainable agriculture.

Present

Thriving Community Hub

Now a 15-acre organic coffee farm, education center, and popular agritourism destination welcoming visitors from around the world.

Our Core Values

Guiding principles that shape everything we do

Environmental Stewardship

We practice regenerative agriculture that enriches our soils, protects water sources, and preserves biodiversity.

Quality & Craftsmanship

From seedling to cup, we take pride in every step of our process, ensuring exceptional quality in every bean.

Community Empowerment

We invest in our workers and neighbors through fair wages, education, and sustainable economic opportunities.

Knowledge Sharing

We believe in preserving traditional wisdom while innovating and sharing our learnings with future generations.
