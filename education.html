Beginner Organic Coffee Farming

1-2 Day Workshops

Perfect for new farmers or those considering transitioning to organic coffee production. Our beginner workshops provide foundational knowledge in a practical, accessible format.

Topics Covered:

  • Introduction to organic coffee farming principles
  • Selecting and preparing land for coffee
  • Choosing appropriate coffee varieties
  • Organic soil management and composting
  • Natural pest and disease control methods
  • Basic shade management and intercropping

Sample Schedule (2-Day Workshop):

Day 1: Classroom & Demonstration
  • 9:00-10:30 Introduction to Organic Principles
  • 10:45-12:30 Soil Health & Composting Demo
  • 1:30-3:00 Coffee Plant Biology & Varieties
  • 3:15-5:00 Farm Walk & Site Evaluation
Day 2: Hands-on Practice
  • 9:00-10:30 Planting & Nursery Management
  • 10:45-12:30 Natural Pest Control Workshop
  • 1:30-3:00 Pruning Techniques
  • 3:15-5:00 Q&A and Certification Overview

Advanced Organic Coffee Farming

5-Day Intensive Program

Course Highlights:

  • Biodynamic farming techniques
  • Integrated pest management systems
  • Water conservation strategies
  • Organic certification process
  • Farm business planning
  • Post-harvest processing methods
  • Marketing organic products

Community Outreach

Supporting Local Farmers

We believe in sharing knowledge to strengthen our agricultural community. Our free outreach programs help smallholder farmers improve their livelihoods through sustainable practices.

Our Outreach Initiatives:

Mobile Training Unit

We bring workshops to rural areas, demonstrating organic techniques farmers can implement immediately.

Starter Kits

Qualified participants receive organic inputs like compost starters and natural pest control solutions.

Farmer Networks

We connect farmers for knowledge sharing and collective marketing opportunities.

Soil Testing

Free basic soil analysis with recommendations for organic amendments.
