Common Questions

You can purchase our coffee through several channels:

  • At the farm: Visit us to buy directly and get the freshest batches
  • Online: Order through our website (coming soon)
  • Nairobi Stockists: Available at select specialty stores
  • Wholesale: Contact David for bulk orders (100kg+)

We're working to expand our distribution network - check back for updates!

Currently, we only ship within Kenya via courier. For international orders:

  • Minimum order of 5kg required due to shipping costs
  • Shipping time varies by destination (typically 7-14 days)
  • Customer responsible for any import duties

Contact David with international inquiries for a shipping quote.

We occasionally accept volunteers, particularly during harvest season (Oct-Jan). Volunteer opportunities depend on:

  • Current farm needs and capacity
  • Your skills and experience
  • Minimum 2-week commitment

Volunteers typically help with harvesting, processing, or educational programs. Send your CV and interest to Grace.

Yes! We offer internships in:

  • Organic farming practices
  • Agroecology and permaculture
  • Sustainable coffee processing
  • Farm-based education
  • Agritourism operations

Internships are typically 3-6 months. University students in relevant fields preferred. Contact Grace for current opportunities.
